Placements at Government College of Engineering, Tirunelveli

GCE Tirunelveli has a placement cell that provides job opportunities to the students who are pursuing undergraduate and postgraduate courses from the college. GCE Tirunelveli placement cell keeps in touch with reputed firms and organisations. Government College of Engineering Tirunelveli placement cell provides training to the students by organising various guest lectures, workshops, mock interviews, etc. GCE Tirunelveli placement cell also organises aptitude and technical test sessions. This training helps the students to perform well in the final Government College of Engineering Tirunelveli placement drive. The placement cell sends invitations to the companies and interested companies will revert back to the GCE Tirunelveli placement cell. Placement dates will be fixed and the companies will visit the campus to conduct pre-placement talk followed by group discussions, tests and interviews. Selected students will be given job offers
